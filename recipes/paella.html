Paella

Description

Originating in the Valencia region, paella is a saffron-infused rice dish cooked in a large, shallow pan. The classic Valencian version is made with chicken, rabbit, and green beans, but seafood paella (paella de marisco) and mixed paella are also very popular.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Meat
  • Beans
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Place the paella pan (paellera) on the heat and add a generous amount of olive oil.
  2. Add the chicken and rabbit pieces to the hot oil. Brown the meat thoroughly on all sides to create a deep, rich flavor.
  3. Push the meat to the side of the pan and add the green beans and lima beans. Saut√© them for a few minutes until they begin to soften.
  4. Create a space in the center of the pan and add the grated tomato and paprika. Cook for a minute, stirring the tomato to create a sofrito, before mixing it with the rest of the ingredients
